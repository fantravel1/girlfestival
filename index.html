Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know
About Women-Only Festivals

What are the best women-only festivals in the world?

The best women-only festivals span every continent and genre — from legendary music gatherings and goddess retreats in North America and Europe, to transformative wellness experiences in Bali and Costa Rica, pioneering adventure festivals in Oceania, and emerging cultural celebrations across Africa and Asia. GirlFestival catalogs 250+ verified women-only festivals across 50+ countries, each vetted for safety, inclusion, and cultural integrity. The "best" festival depends on what you're seeking — music, healing, leadership, adventure, or spiritual connection.

Are there music festivals just for women?

Yes — women-only music festivals exist worldwide and are growing in number and diversity. These range from large outdoor multi-day festivals featuring all-women lineups to intimate acoustic gatherings and songwriting retreats. Women-only music festivals create spaces where female artists headline by default and audiences experience live music free from the safety concerns and power dynamics common at mixed events. You'll find everything from rock and folk to electronic, world music, and classical.

How safe are women-only festivals compared to mixed events?

Women-only festivals consistently report dramatically lower rates of harassment, assault, and safety incidents compared to mixed-gender events. This is by design — with explicit consent cultures, trained safety teams, clear reporting systems, and community accountability. Attendees consistently describe feeling a level of freedom and safety they rarely experience elsewhere. GirlFestival evaluates every event's safety infrastructure across seven dimensions before listing.

Can I attend a women-only festival alone safely?

Attending a women-only festival solo is one of the safest and most rewarding ways to experience these events. Many attendees come alone and find instant community. Most women-only festivals are specifically designed to welcome solo travelers with buddy systems, welcome circles, and first-timer orientations. GirlFestival marks events that are "first-timer friendly" and "solo-traveler safe" to help you find your best fit.

Are women-only events inclusive of queer women?

Many women-only festivals explicitly welcome queer, trans, and nonbinary attendees. GirlFestival includes a "queer-inclusive" filter so you can find events that actively celebrate LGBTQ+ identities. Each event's inclusion policy is clearly listed so you can choose spaces where you'll feel fully seen and welcomed. The women-only festival movement has deep roots in queer culture and community.

Are women-only festivals sober-friendly?

Many women-only festivals offer sober-friendly or fully alcohol-free environments. GirlFestival lets you filter for sober-friendly events specifically. These range from fully substance-free wellness retreats to festivals that offer robust alcohol-free programming and designated sober spaces alongside general festivities. Sobriety is respected and supported — never questioned.

Are there women-only wellness retreats worldwide?

Women-only wellness retreats are available on every inhabited continent. These include yoga and meditation retreats, holistic healing gatherings, fitness and outdoor wellness festivals, trauma-informed therapeutic retreats, and integrative health programs. Popular destinations include Bali, Costa Rica, Portugal, Morocco, India, and the mountains of Colorado and British Columbia. Many are designed to be accessible to first-timers and solo travelers.

Are there women-only festivals for teens or college-age women?

Yes — there are women-only festivals and gatherings designed specifically for teens, college-age women, and young adults. These include coming-of-age ceremonies, rites-of-passage retreats, young women's leadership summits, and empowerment festivals. GirlFestival lets you filter events by life stage so you can find age-appropriate, empowering gatherings that meet young women exactly where they are.

Are there women-only retreats for mothers?

Women-only retreats for mothers are among the fastest-growing segments in the festival world. These range from "mom getaway" wellness weekends to deep, facilitated retreats addressing postpartum healing, identity beyond motherhood, and reconnection with self. Some welcome children; others are intentionally child-free to give mothers uninterrupted space. GirlFestival marks which events are family-friendly and which offer dedicated time for mothers alone.

How do women-only festivals enforce safety and consent?

Women-only festivals enforce safety through multiple layers: trained on-site safety teams, explicit consent guidelines shared before and during events, anonymous reporting systems, community accountability practices, clear alcohol and substance policies, medical access, and well-lit, thoughtfully designed physical spaces. The women-only environment itself is the foundation — creating a baseline of mutual respect and shared understanding that transforms how everyone experiences the event.
